# Carousel — Support

Carousel turns your iPad into a calm family hearth: it slowly drifts
through your own photos, quotes, reminders, and the day's calendar events — one
at a time.

## Quick help

**Nothing shows up / it looks empty.**
Open the first-run guide or tap the screen and use the pencil (Compose) to add a
few photos or quotes. To show the day's events, tap the gear (Settings) and turn
on Calendar and/or Reminders.

**My photos aren't appearing.**
Carousel needs access to your photo library. Tap the gear → enable Photos, or
open iOS Settings → Carousel → Photos and allow access. If you granted access to
only a few photos, choose "All Photos" to browse albums.

**Calendar events or reminders aren't showing.**
Tap the gear and make sure Calendar / Reminders are on, and that the right
calendars and lists are selected. The display shows events from the next few
days and reminders that are due soon.

**A portrait photo has a head cut off.**
By default Carousel shows the whole photo on a soft blurred background and never
crops. If you turned on "smart crop" to fill the screen, it uses on-device vision
to keep faces in frame — turn it off in Settings to always show the whole photo.

**It dims at night.**
That's intentional — Carousel brightens by day and dims in the evening. You can
adjust day/night behavior in Settings.
